Booma Reader

Booma Readers is an Arabic book reading app designed to spark a lifelong love of learning. With a rich catalog of fiction and non-fiction, our engaging and joyful platform makes reading easy to navigate and fun to explore. We empower children through achievements and progress tracking, while giving parents the tools to follow their child’s journey every step of the way.

Booma Kids Academy

Booma Kids Academy is an Arabic‑first early‑learning platform that turns play into real progress. Through short, joyful activities in language, math, science, and the arts, children build strong foundations step by step, at their own pace. We celebrate every small win and give parents simple, transparent tools to follow growth day by day.
